Rambag Palace


Nestled in the vibrant outskirts of Maharashtra, the Ramabg Wedding 
Venue promises an enchanting escape for couples seeking timeless 
celebrations. Boasting a grand banquet hall and lush garden, it 
offers a picturesque setting for weddings. Luxurious accommodation
ensures comfort for guests, while customizable packages cater to 
every need. With scenic surroundings and professional event planning,
this venue creates unforgettable moments amidst the beauty of nature
and Rajasthan's rich heritage.
  • Max people: 100-1500
  • Food per plate: 500-5000
  • Location: 567 Vineyard Lane, Nashik, Maharashtra
